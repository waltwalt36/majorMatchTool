COMPUTER SCIENCE

If you enjoy programming, a major in Computer Science is an excellent choice. This field combines theoretical foundations with practical skills, allowing you to delve into algorithms, data structures, software development, and systems design. You'll learn to write efficient code, solve complex problems, and create innovative software solutions. Additionally, computer science offers numerous opportunities to work on team projects, whether in collaborative coding sessions, group research initiatives, or software development teams. This major will equip you with the technical skills and collaborative experience needed to thrive in the tech industry, where teamwork and problem-solving are crucial.
